About Us

Welcome to Foo's Pies, your ultimate destination for mouthwatering pizzas! Since our establishment, we have been dedicated to serving the finest and most delicious pies to our valued customers.

At Foo's Pies, we believe in quality and taste above all else. Our pizzas are meticulously crafted using only the freshest and highest-quality ingredients, ensuring each bite is an explosion of flavors.

Our dedicated team of expert chefs puts their heart and soul into every pizza they make. From classic favorites like Pepperoni and Cheese to creative delights like Mushroom and Sausage, we have a pizza to suit every palate.

Ordering from Foo's Pies is a breeze. Whether you choose to visit our cozy location on Main Street in Des Moines or prefer the convenience of ordering online, we promise to deliver a pizza experience like no other.

Customer satisfaction is our top priority, and we go the extra mile to make every visit or delivery a delightful one. We take pride in being a part of your special moments, from family gatherings to game nights and everything in between.

Thank you for choosing Foo's Pies. We are incredibly grateful for your continued support, and we look forward to serving you many more delicious pizzas in the future!
